ISKOLAR APP
Scholarship Partnership and Student Assistance Platform


OVERVIEW:

ISKOLAR APP is a web-based scholarship management and partnership platform designed to connect schools, scholarship providers, and students in one centralized digital ecosystem.

Unlike generic scholarship listing platforms, ISKOLAR APP focuses on school-partnered scholarship management, where universities and colleges collaborate directly with scholarship providers such as government agencies, NGOs, private institutions, and foundations.

The platform streamlines scholarship application, review, approval, monitoring, and student support processes. It also includes features for student assistance requests, scholarship tracking, and institutional coordination.

The goal of ISKOLAR APP is to make scholarship opportunities more accessible, transparent, organized, and efficient for all stakeholders involved.

PROBLEM STATEMENT:

1. Limited accessibility to secure and transparent donation platforms
2. Limited reach of financial aid options for underserved students
3. Inconsistent academic performance among students.

OBJECTIVES:

General Objective: Improved access to transparent donation platforms with at least 90% user availability.

Specific Objective 1: Faster access to scholarship updates and announcements among students within 24 hours of posting.

Specific Objective 2: Reduced levels of academic and financial stress among students to below 40% of the population.

CUSTOMER PERSONA:

1. Students (Iskolar)
Description:Students who are seeking scholarships, financial assistance, or educational support.

Needs:
    Find available scholarships
    Submit scholarship applications online
    Track application status
    Upload requirements digitally
    Request assistance through the “Post a Need” feature

Goals:
    Obtain financial support for education
    Receive updates and notifications efficiently
    Access legitimate scholarship opportunities

2. School Administrators
Description : University or college personnel responsible for managing scholarship partnerships and student applications.

Needs:
    Review and verify applications
    Manage scholarship records
    Coordinate with scholarship providers
    Monitor student compliance and eligibility

Goals
    Simplify scholarship management
    Reduce manual processing
    Improve institutional coordination

3. Scholarship Providers
Description : Government agencies, private organizations, NGOs, or foundations offering scholarship programs.

Needs
    Partner with schools
    Publish scholarship programs
    Review applicants
    Monitor scholar performance and reports

Goals
    Efficiently distribute scholarship grants
    Ensure qualified student selection
